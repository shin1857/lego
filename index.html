The LEGO History

Founded in 1932 by Ole Kirk Kristiansen, and based on the iconic LEGO brick,
it is one of the world’s leading manufacturers of play materials.

20,400+ people in 40+ countries

Working at the LEGO Group is more than just fun: it’s exciting, inspiring, and filled with creativity.

Supporting a more creative and resilient society

LEGO Foundation website

The LEGO Foundation is helping build a future in which learning through play empowers children to become creative, engaged, lifelong learners.

LEGO Education website

LEGO® Education inspires the students of today to be the smart, creative, and STEAM-minded leaders of tomorrow.

LEGO House website

LEGO® House, "Home of the Brick", is the ultimate experience centre based in Billund, Denmark - 12,000m² large and filled with 25 million LEGO® bricks.

LEGO ventures website

LEGO Ventures invests in the future of learning, creativity, and play. play is critical to growth and development, stimulating children’s imagination and creative expression.
